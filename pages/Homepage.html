WHERE ARE WE ?
Artixium Display is represented by two European visionaries who understood that LED technology would take a big part in the digital revolution of our century, changing people’s lives and their cities. Since 2012 and Artixium Display ‘s creation in Hong Kong with manufacturing facilities in Shenzhen and Wuhan, we’ve been evolving and always looking for innovative ways to contribute to the digital transition of our world. It only took a few years for Artixium to become a key player in the Asian LED display industry while being locally represented in Europe. Artixium kept evolving throughout the years which led to a strategic industrial partnership with Liantronics Group.
